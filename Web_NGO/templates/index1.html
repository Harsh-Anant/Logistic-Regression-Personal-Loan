Replenishing life in the “Dead”

Few eyes hint at a lost promise or offer a glimmer of hope. It’s easy to walk past those people but it’s tough to forget those eyes once caught up with yours.Our project is a mission, dedicated to all those millions of homeless men and women struggling to live under a roof, crying mothers suffering through the misery of looking at their children screaming out of hunger, the fathers selling their skin to protect the skin of their family, to everyone trapped in the viscous cycle of “helplessness” also known as poverty.

OUR MISSION

The Givers




You can, if you want and you have


Our project has two categories, The first one being for the people who want to donate, and have clothes, food or any form of shelter that they can provide for the poor to use or to any other person working for the welfare of the underprivileged can register here and donate.



You can, if you want but don’t have


This is the second category of our project for those who want to help but can’t donate,they can register here and help the dependent in being independent by bringing them to this platform.Donating is certainly one of the many forms of help but help comes in many forms.
